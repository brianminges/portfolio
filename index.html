My story

How a 20-year career in the media industry prepared me to be a software developer.

Finding missing punctuation. Editing verbose copy. Hitting crazy deadlines. It sounds like software development, but for me, it's how I spent more than 20 years in the media industry. My career as a copy editor and page designer took me all across the country. As the industry changed, my work began to include more website responsibilities. What started as just posting stories via our content management system evolved into deep dives into social media and search engine optimization. It was while working with SEO that I got my first look under the hood of web browsers. I was fascinated. And hooked. I knew I needed more. I was accepted into a software development bootcamp at Nashville Software School and thus started my formal education of writing code. JavaScript. React. Python. One year later, here I am. Still finding missing punctuation. Still editing verbose copy. Still hitting crazy deadlines. And loving every second of it.
